Your guide to frequently asked questions

What is the company's mission?
Our mission is to deliver exceptional products and services to our clients while maintaining a strong commitment to sustainability.
How do I request time off?
You can request time off through the employee portal. Please submit at least two weeks in advance.
Where can I find the employee handbook?
The employee handbook is available in the "Documents" section of the employee portal.
Who do I contact for IT support?
You can contact IT support via the "Help" section in the employee portal or by emailing it-support@company.com.
How do I enroll in benefits?
You can enroll in benefits during the open enrollment period via the employee portal.
What is the company's dress code?
Our dress code is business casual. Please dress appropriately for client meetings and special events.
How do I reset my password?
You can reset your password by going to the login page and clicking "Forgot Password."
What is the company's policy on remote work?
The company allows remote work one day per week with manager approval.
How do I update my contact information?
You can update your contact information through the "Profile" section of the employee portal.
